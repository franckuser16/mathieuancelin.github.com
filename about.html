I'm a software engineer at SERLI and I'm specialized in Java EE technologies with a strong interest on lightweight component frameworks. I'm also involved in several open-source projects such as JOnAS and GlassFish, and even leads some like Weld-OSGi, RS-MVC and Dependency-Shot

I'm a JSR-346 (CDI 1.1) expert group member and a crew member of the Poitou-Charentes JUG.

I'm a regular speaker on Java EE related topics (JavaOne, Solutions Linux, JUGSummerCamp, DevoxxFR as well as in some JUG events) and I give lectures at the University of La Rochelle and University of Poitiers.
